Sunny-side Up Eggs

To make sunny-side up eggs, first turn the stove on medium heat. After a few minutes, put a tablespoon of vegetable oil (or your choice of cooking oil) on the pan. Spread the oil on the pan and allow it to heat up.

Next, crack the egg on the side of the pan and empty the egg onto the hot pan. Cook the egg for 3-5 minutes.

Lastly, sprinkle the seasoning of your choice on top of the egg. With the spatula, scoop the egg off of the pan and onto your plate to eat. Enjoy!
